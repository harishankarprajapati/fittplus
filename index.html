FITT Plus comes in the origin by dreaming of two fitness enthusiast Mr. Palash Biswas and Ms Tilu Jain who are the Founder Director’s and named their Dream as FITT PLUS , its comes from  F I T T  Principle (or formula) is a great way of monitoring your exercise program. The acronym FITT outlines the key components, or training guidelines, for an effective exercise program, and the initials F, I, T, T, stand for: Frequency, Intensity, Time and Type.
