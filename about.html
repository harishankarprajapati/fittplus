ABOUT

FITT Plus comes to the origin by dreaming of two fitness enthusiast Mr. Palash Biswas and Mrs Tilu Jain who are the Founder Director’s and named their Dream as FITT PLUS , it comes from F I T T Principle, it is a great way of monitoring your fitness regime. The acronym FITT outlines the key components or training guidelines for an effective exercise program and the initials F. I. T. T. Stands for:Frequency, Intensity, Time and Type.

Join Now

Frequency: Refers to the frequency of exercise undertaken or how often you workout.

Intensity: Intensity stands for hardcore workout regime you follow.

Time: The time you spend exercising or how long you exercise for.

Type: The type of exercise undertaken or what kind of exercise you do.
